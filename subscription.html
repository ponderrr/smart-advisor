Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work? +
For monthly subscriptions, you'll be billed every month on the same date you initially subscribed. For annual plans, you'll be billed once per year. You can cancel your subscription at any time.
Can I switch between plans? +
Yes, you can switch between plans at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference immediately. If you downgrade, your new plan will take effect at your next billing cycle.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. After cancellation, you'll still have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), as well as PayPal for subscription payments.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not satisfied with our premium service within the first 14 days, contact our support team for a full refund.
